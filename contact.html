The Belgian Puppet Users Group uses different sites and channels to organize, promote and report about meetings and meetups organized by the puppet User Group.

How to become a member of the Belgian Puppet User Group ?

Becoming a member of this group is done using the Belgian Puppet Users Group Meetup Site and click on the 'join' button. You will have to create a free meetup account to be able to join. The Belgian Puppet Users Group Meetup Site site is also used to shedule our meetups, and members can notify the group if they will attend a meetup or not.

How can I contact one of the organisers ?

Use the Contact Organiser link on the Belgian Puppet Users Group Meetup Site. Remember that you must be a registred meetup member.

Join us on IRC at the #puppet-be channel on Freenode.

You can contact me directly at johan-AT-open-future.be, please add BPUG in your subject
