Contacting the Belgian Puppet User Group

The Belgian Puppet User Group uses different sites and channels to organize, promote and report about meetings and meetups organized by the puppet User Group.

How to become a member of the Belgian Puppet User Group ?

Becoming a member of this group is done using the [Belgian Puppet Group Meetup] and click on the 'join' button. You will have to create a free meetup account to be able to join. The [Belgian Puppet Group Meetup](http://www.meetup.com/Belgian-Puppet-User-Group/) site is also used to shedule our meetups , and where members will tell if they are attending a meetup or not.

How can I contact one of the organisers ?

Use the [Contact Organiser link](http://www.meetup.com/Belgian-Puppet-User-Group/suggestion/) ath the Belgian Pupept Usere Group meetup website. Remember that you must be a registred meetup member.

Join us on IRC at the #puppet-be channel on [freenode](http://freenode.net).

You can contact me directly at johan-AT-open-future.be, and please add BPUG in your subject
